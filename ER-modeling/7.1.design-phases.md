# Design Phases

- **1 Phase** :
  - The outcome of this phase is a **specification of user requirements**.
- **2 Phase** :
  - The schema developedat **this conceptual-design** phase provides a detailed overview of the enterprise. Basically develop ER Model here.
  - focus at this point is on describing the data and their relationships,rather than on specifying physical storage details.

- **3 Phase**:
  - the designer can review the schema to ensure it meets functional requirements.
